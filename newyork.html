Eggs Benedict
Eggs Benedict, also informally known as Eggs Benny, is a traditional American breakfast or brunch dish that consists of two halves of an warm, toasted English muffin topped with a poached egg, bacon or ham, and a zesty hollandaise sauce. The dish was first popularized in New York City. Many variations on the basic recipe are served.
