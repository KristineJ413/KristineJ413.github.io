Açaí bowls
A colorful mix of Brazil's native açaí berries with juiced and frozen fruit, served as a smoothie in a bowl or glass. Do as the locals do and add toppings, like granola, seeds and banana, to your heart's content. Açaí bowls are now a popular snack in other parts of the world, like California.
